About Ashley

Raised by a single mother who is a nurse practitioner, working in the medical field runs in Ashley’s family. She’s never had problems making friends, and enjoys being the center of attention. Ashley is currently studying at Westfield university on a partial scholarship. She’s studying to become an ER nurse but is also double majoring in forensic science. On campus she has her three closest friends from highschool that live in the same dorm complex. Ashley and her friends love to go out on the weekends, attending frat parties at neighboring schools and going to clubs. She’s never been much of a drinker, and does not partake in a lot of the use of substances that her friends seem to be so fond of. Her friends are very belligerent and love to be extremely intoxicated at every weekend function, but Ashley is always sober enough to take care of them, and nurse their hangovers. She’s often seen as the motherly figure of their group because of this, and Ashley enjoys watching her friends make fools of themselves every weekend. Her cannibalistic cravings always seem to creep back when she’s babysitting her drunk friends. As Ashley has become more reserved since starting college, her friends always wonder what happened to the loud and extroverted girl she was in highschool. Studying the human body as well as forensic science has changed her view on the people she has always been surrounded by. Now, as a junior in college, she finds pleasure in observing how those intoxicated bodies react to different stimuli, often observing the girls’ behavior on the weekends through a scientific lens, while trying to quiet her cravings for flesh.
